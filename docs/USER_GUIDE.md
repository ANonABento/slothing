# Slothing User Guide

Welcome to Slothing, your AI-powered job application assistant.

## Getting Started

### 1. Set Up Your Profile

Navigate to **My Profile** in the sidebar to enter your information:

- Contact details (name, email, phone)
- Professional summary
- Work experience
- Education
- Skills

A complete profile helps generate better tailored resumes and interview prep.

### 2. Upload Your Resume

Go to **Upload Resume** to import your existing resume:

- Supports PDF and common document formats
- AI extracts your information automatically
- Review and edit extracted data in your profile

### 3. Add Target Opportunities

In **Opportunities**, add job descriptions you're applying to:

- Paste the full job description
- Slothing extracts requirements automatically
- Track status: Saved → Applied → Interviewing → Offered

---

## Key Features

### Resume Tailoring

For each opportunity, Slothing can generate a tailored resume:

1. Go to **Opportunities** and select an opportunity
2. Click **Generate Resume**
3. Choose a template style
4. Download as PDF

The AI emphasizes skills and experience matching the job requirements.

### ATS Score Analysis

See how well your resume matches an opportunity:

1. Select an opportunity in **Opportunities**
2. Click **Analyze Match**
3. Review your match score
4. See skill gaps and suggestions

### Interview Prep

Practice for interviews with AI-generated questions:

1. Go to **Interview Prep**
2. Select an opportunity
3. Choose **Text Practice** or **Voice Practice**
4. Answer questions and get instant feedback

**Voice Mode:**

- Questions are read aloud
- Speak your answers using the microphone
- Transcription converts speech to text
- Record your answers for playback

### Cover Letters

Generate customized cover letters:

1. In **Opportunities**, select an opportunity
2. Click **Cover Letter**
3. Review and edit the generated letter
4. Copy or download

### Email Templates

Create professional emails for:

- Follow-ups
- Thank you notes
- Networking outreach
- Salary negotiation

Go to **Email Templates**, select a type, and generate.

### Salary Tools

Research and negotiate salaries:

1. **Salary Calculator**: Get market rates for your role and location
2. **Compare Offers**: Analyze multiple opportunity offers side-by-side
3. **Negotiation Script**: Generate talking points for salary discussions

### Calendar & Reminders

Track deadlines and interviews:

- View upcoming events in **Calendar**
- Set reminders for follow-ups
- Export to Google Calendar or Outlook

### Analytics

Track your opportunity search progress in **Analytics**:

- Applications by status
- Profile completeness
- Interview conversion rate
- Export data for your records

## Tips for Success

### Complete Your Profile

A 100% complete profile leads to better tailored content.

### Use Specific Job Descriptions

Paste the full opportunity posting, including requirements and responsibilities.

### Practice Regularly

Use Interview Prep before every interview. Practice makes perfect.

### Track Everything

Log every application in Opportunities to see patterns in your success.

### Follow Up

Use Email Templates to send thank you notes within 24 hours of interviews.

## Settings

### LLM Provider

Configure your AI provider in **Settings**:

- OpenAI (recommended)
- Anthropic Claude
- OpenRouter
- Ollama (local, free)

You need at least one provider configured for AI features.

### Theme

Toggle between Light, Dark, and System themes using the theme button in the sidebar.

### Keyboard Shortcuts

Press `?` anywhere to see available keyboard shortcuts.

---

## Troubleshooting

### AI Features Not Working

- Check that you've configured an LLM provider in Settings
- Verify your API key is valid
- Check the browser console for errors

### Resume Upload Failed

- Ensure the file is a valid PDF
- Try a smaller file size
- Check that the PDF contains text (not scanned images)

### Interview Voice Mode Issues

- Allow microphone access in your browser
- Use Chrome or Edge for best compatibility
- Check that your microphone is working

---

## Privacy

Slothing stores all data locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers except:

- LLM requests for AI features (sent to your configured provider)

Your resume, profile, and job applications remain private on your device.
