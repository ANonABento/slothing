# [MKT] Marketing footer "Resources" links point at protected app routes (broken UX for logged-out visitors)

## Problem

The marketing footer "Resources" column linked logged-out visitors to authenticated app routes such as `/dashboard`, `/bank`, and `/interview`.

## Fix

Keep footer resources on public marketing/legal routes only, and remove or replace links to non-existent public pages.
