# [LEGAL] Privacy & Terms pages missing required sections (rights, contact, cookies, jurisdiction)

## Problem

Both `/en/privacy` and `/en/terms` are extremely short (under 5 sections each) and missing legally important content that any commercial product collecting user data and integrating with Google OAuth needs:

**Privacy missing:**
- User rights section (access, correction, deletion, portability, objection — required under GDPR/CCPA)
- A real contact email / address (current text: "directed through the support channel associated with your workspace" — unusable)
- Cookies / local storage disclosure (the product clearly uses storage; see `apps/web/src/lib/extension/install.ts` Storage permission description)
- Children's policy (COPPA — no statement about under-13 use)
- International transfers (especially relevant since Google services and LLM providers are mentioned)
- Security practices (even a high-level statement)
- Data retention specifics — current "active workspace" wording doesn't say how long deleted/backed-up data lives
- Last-updated date matches "Effective March 25, 2026" but no change-log link

**Terms missing:**
- Governing law / jurisdiction (literally cannot enforce ToS without it)
- Account termination policy (when can you cancel; when can we close your account)
- Payment / billing terms (Pro tier exists on `/en/pricing` for $8/mo, ToS says nothing about billing, refunds, taxes, auto-renew)
- IP / content ownership (who owns the AI-generated resume?)
- Indemnification + warranty disclaimer (only one weak "as-is" sentence)
- Acceptable use policy (no rules against abuse, automation, reselling)
- Dispute resolution / arbitration
